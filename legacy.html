How I want to be remembered . .
My mark on Hart . .
What I stood for. The energy I brought . .

I may be remembered as the Christian. I may be remembered as the guy in sandals. I may be remembered as the Guatemalan, the one who was always wearing sweaters, the one with the weird voice, etc.. The way I want to be remembered however, is as the one who made a positive impact-- big or small. If that's what I'm known for, then I'm a happy camper!

Advice to future Hart students . .
What I'd tell my freshman-self . .
Things I've learned . .
1.Don't wait for permission to be passionate.
2.Believe and trust in those older than you.
3.Put any given moment into perspective. You're young; recognize that.
4.Be picky about those who you let be around you. They'll shape you way, way more than expected.
